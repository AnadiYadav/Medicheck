About MediCheck

Welcome to the website. This is an original website created for the purpose of providing valid information regarding medicines used by people in the ordinary course of life.

This website focuses on providing valid information regarding medicines available. Many people take drugs without knowing their actual use. This website will assist them in finding out the correct information about any kind of medicine.

This website is committed to its values regarding the authenticity of the information, and the correct source of information.

How to Use Our Website

Step 1: Click on "Get Started"

To begin, click on the "Get Started" button from the navigation menu. This will redirect you to the main page where you can start the identification process.

Step 2: Upload or Click Picture of Medicine Wrapper

On the next page, you will be given two options: either you can upload an image of the medicine wrapper or click a picture using your device's camera. This is done through a simple dropdown menu.

Go to the Page

Step 3: Click on "Search"

After selecting or uploading the image, simply click on the "Search" button. Our system will process the image and show the result in a few moments.

Step 4: Contact Us If Not Satisfied

If the results are not what you expected or if you need more assistance, feel free to reach out to us by clicking on the "Contact Us" section on the homepage.

What is MediCheck, and how can it help me?
MediCheck is a platform designed to provide accurate and reliable information about medicines. It helps users understand the proper use, dosage, and side effects of medicines, ensuring safe usage.
How does MediCheck identify medicines?
MediCheck uses an image-based recognition system. You can upload or click a picture of a medicine wrapper, and the system will process the image to provide detailed information about the medicine.
Is the information on MediCheck reliable?
Yes, MediCheck is committed to providing authentic and verified information sourced from credible and trustworthy references.
What should I do if the search result doesn’t match my medicine?
If the result isn’t accurate or doesn’t meet your expectations, you can contact us through the “Contact Us” section on the homepage for assistance.
Can I use MediCheck on my mobile device?
Absolutely! MediCheck is compatible with both desktop and mobile devices, allowing users to upload or take pictures directly from their smartphones.
Is there a charge for using MediCheck?
MediCheck is currently free to use. Users can access all features, including searching for medicines and contacting our team, without any charges.
